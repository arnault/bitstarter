The code of the genome has been cracked for a few years and, of course, it has brought us amazing new knowledge on the biological world. But the fact is we contemplate now how much we still do not know: from the DNA to a fully developed human brain, the chain of causes and effects are still almost wrapped up in darkness. If we want to understand better the stuff we are made of and how the human experience emerge, we need to bring all the data together and mine it as if there will be no tomorrow.

We want to build this App in order to answer to that need. We want to offer a platform where parents can record the data relating to their child, to inform themselves on up-to-date scientific theories concerning the development of their child. We want to offer the parents an interesting and seriously playful platform where they will join a community of parents looking ot have a reflective thinking on parenthood. We want to offer the experts - psychologist, linguist, MDs, philosophers, etc. - the bi ggest pool of data that can be gathered in the era of the internet. This App will build a bridge between the experiences of parents and the knowledge of experts.

Once the child gets older, he will be able to access a platform where he can play games and relate to things that his development needs at the time. Raising a kid is hard work. A mother and a father are lost in a sea of new information. If parents have more than one kid, they will get better at it. But this wealth of experience is lost after that. Maybe the child, once an adult will reflect on this experience when he will have child of his or her own. But the fact is that it is done always in a the urgency of the situation. What we aim, is that to create a community of concern around the child which is immensely bigger that the parents. That way, when the good news hits you, you can say: "Well, I won't be alone, there will be MediChild.
